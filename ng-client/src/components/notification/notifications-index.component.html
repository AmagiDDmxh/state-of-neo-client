Notifications are events raised by smart contract executions. They can be used by developers or outside apps to receive information directly from the Smart Contract that is being invoked on the network.

Notifications info

For example when a NEP-5 token is transferred, a "transfer" notification is being raised by its smart contract. This notification carry information about what amount was transferred and who were the sender and the recipient of the transaction. This can be handy if you are building an exchange for example and you want to detect when tokens were deposited to a user's account. Or if simply you are participating in an ICO and would like to know if your tokens were transferred to your name. Using this section you will be able to know this immediately.
